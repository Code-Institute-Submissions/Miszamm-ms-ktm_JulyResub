What is KTM?

KTM AG (formerly KTM Sportmotorcycle AG) is an Austrian motorcycle and sports car manufacturer owned by Pierer Mobility AG. It was formed in 1992 but traces its foundation to as early as 1934. Today, KTM AG is the parent company of the KTM. Group, consisting of a number of motorcycle brands.

Why KTM?

KTM is known for its off-road motorcycles. Since the late 1990s, it has expanded into street motorcycle production and developing sport cars. KTM sold almost as many street as off-road bikes. Stylish looks and great features, amazing engine which gives speed - amazing speed, better reliability and great performance. KTM bikes also offers easy customization with its accessories being easily available.
